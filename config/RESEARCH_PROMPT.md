# Research Prompt Used by AI PowerPoint Generator

This is the **EXACT** research prompt that the application sends to Google Gemini when generating presentations.

---

## Full Research Prompt Template

```
Role: Act as a {TOPIC} Distinguished Professor and Subject Matter Specialist with decades of experience in {TOPIC}. Your writing style is academic yet accessible, prioritizing "Granular Detail" over general summaries.

Task: Generate a comprehensive, 5,000-word Master Thesis-level content guide for a presentation on "{TOPIC}".

CRITICAL EXPANSION REQUIREMENTS (The 5000-Word Rule):

1. Mini-Essay Bullets: Do NOT provide simple bullet points. Each bullet point must be a 250-300 word detailed paragraph.

2. The "Three-Layer" Rule: For every fact mentioned, you must provide: (1) The Statement, (2) The underlying Mechanism/Proof, and (3) A Real-world Case Study or Data Point.

3. Numerical Density: Every section must contain at least 5 specific data points (percentages, dates, molar masses, financial figures, or coordinates).

4. Temporal Relevance: You must weave in developments from 2024, 2025, and early 2026 to ensure the content is at the absolute "edge" of the field.

REQUIRED STRUCTURE (8 Modules):

Module 1: Exhaustive Taxonomy & Historical Genesis
- Provide a 500-word definition covering etymology and technical boundaries
- Detail the "Pre-Discovery" era vs. "Post-Discovery" impact
- Analyze the 2026 global relevance and socio-economic/biological footprint
- Include at least 5 specific dates, statistics, or numerical data points

Module 2: Theoretical Architecture & Core Mechanics
- Explain the 5-7 fundamental laws or principles governing this topic
- Deep-dive into the "Molecular/Structural" level of how it functions
- Contrast this topic against its 3 closest "competitors" or similar concepts
- Provide mechanistic explanations with specific biochemical/physical/technical details

Module 3: The "Process" Micro-Analysis
- A step-by-step chronological breakdown. If it's a lifecycle, provide hourly/daily changes. If it's a system, provide millisecond latency or data flow details
- Identify "Failure Points": Where does the process usually break down?
- Include temporal data (hours, days, weeks) and quantitative measurements
- Explain each phase with 250-300 word detailed paragraphs

Module 4: Practical Manifestations & Data Signatures
- How do we see this in the real world? Provide specific "Field Observations"
- Categorize manifestations into: Primary, Secondary, and Latent (Hidden)
- Include specific symptoms, signs, or observable features with severity classifications
- Provide diagnostic criteria with specific values and thresholds

Module 5: Advanced Diagnostics & Forensic Analysis
- List every modern tool used to measure/identify this (from CRISPR/Mass-Spec to AI-Modeling/Satellite Imaging)
- Compare 2024 methods vs. 2026 "Next-Gen" methods
- Include specific test names, sensitivity/specificity values, and procedural details
- Explain differential diagnosis with specific distinguishing features

Module 6: Management, Intervention, & Optimization Strategies
- Detailed "Protocol" for handling the topic
- If medical: dosages/pathways with specific drug names and mechanisms. If technical: code/architectures. If social: policy/frameworks
- Include a "Crisis Management" subsection for when things go wrong
- Provide step-by-step protocols with specific parameters and expected outcomes
- Include latest 2024-2026 treatment developments with clinical trial data

Module 7: The Research Frontier (2024–2026)
- Cite 3 specific "Current Studies" from 2024-2026 with methodology and findings
- Analyze the "Unsolved Mysteries" still baffling experts this year
- The role of Emerging Tech (Quantum, AI, Bio-engineering) in this specific field
- Include breakthrough discoveries with specific data and implications

Module 8: Legacy, Ethics, & Future Projections
- What is the 50-year outlook with specific predictions?
- Detailed ethical considerations (Environmental, Social, or Biological impact)
- A final "Summary Matrix" of the most critical data points
- Practical implications for society/healthcare/industry

MANDATORY FORMAT FOR EACH MODULE:
- Each bullet point = 250-300 word detailed paragraph (NOT a single sentence)
- Include minimum 5 specific numbers/dates/statistics per module
- Provide real-world examples and case studies
- Explain mechanisms, not just descriptions
- Use the "Three-Layer" approach: Statement → Mechanism → Evidence

Generate comprehensive, thesis-level content for all 8 modules now.
```

---

## Important Notes

- **{TOPIC}** is replaced with your entered topic (e.g., "Artificial Intelligence")
- This prompt is designed to get **MAXIMUM DETAIL** from Gemini
- The 8-module structure ensures comprehensive coverage
- Each module requires **250-300 word paragraphs**, not simple bullets
- Emphasis on **2024-2026 data** for cutting-edge content

---

## Why This Prompt Works

1. **Role Definition**: Sets Gemini as an expert professor
2. **Detail Requirements**: Forces granular, not superficial, content
3. **Three-Layer Rule**: Ensures depth (Statement → Mechanism → Evidence)
4. **Numerical Density**: Guarantees specific data, not vague claims
5. **8-Module Structure**: Covers topic from all angles
6. **Temporal Relevance**: Ensures latest 2024-2026 research
