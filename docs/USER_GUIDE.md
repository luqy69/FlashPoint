## Quick Start

### For Users Without Python

1. **Download** the `package` folder
2. **Double-click** `PPT_Generator.exe`
3. **Follow the wizard** steps
4. **Find your presentations** in the `output` folder

---

## System Requirements

- **Operating System**: Windows 10/11
- **Microsoft PowerPoint**: Required for PPT and PDF export
- **Internet Connection**: Required for AI research
- **Google Account**: Must be logged into Google Gemini

---

## First Time Setup

### Google Gemini Login

The first time you run the app, you'll need to log in to Google Gemini:

1. When prompted, a Chrome browser window will open
2. Log in to your Google account if not already logged in
3. Navigate to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com)
4. Once logged in, close the browser or return to the app
5. The app will remember your login for future use

---

## Using the Wizard

### Step 1: Welcome
- Read the introduction
- Click **Next** to continue

### Step 2: Topic
- Enter your presentation topic
- Or choose from the examples
- Click **Next**

### Step 3: Theme
- Browse 10 professional PowerPoint themes
- Select your preferred theme
- Click **Next**

### Step 4: Export
- Choose export formats:
  - **PPTX** (always included)
  - **PPT** (optional - for older PowerPoint)
  - **PDF** (optional - for sharing)
- Click **Next** to start generation

### Step 5: Progress
- Watch the AI work in real-time
- This typically takes 2-5 minutes
- Do not close the window

### Step 6: Complete
- View your generated files
- Click **Open Folder** to see outputs
- Click **Finish** to exit

---

## Output Files

Your presentations are saved in `package/output/[TopicName]_[Timestamp]/`:

- `[Topic]_presentation.pptx` - Modern PowerPoint file
- `[Topic]_presentation.ppt` - Legacy PowerPoint (if selected)
- `[Topic]_presentation.pdf` - PDF version (if selected)
- `[Topic]_supplemental_research.docx` - Research document
- `charts/` - Downloaded visualizations

## Troubleshooting

### "Gemini Login Required" Error
**Solution**: Open Chrome, go to gemini.google.com, and log in

### "PowerPoint Not Found" Error
**Solution**: Install Microsoft PowerPoint (required for PPT/PDF export)

### "No Internet Connection" Error
**Solution**: Check your internet connection and try again

### Generation Stuck at "Researching..."
**Solution**: 
1. Wait 2-3 minutes (Gemini takes time)
2. If still stuck, restart the app
3. Ensure Gemini account is active

### Executable Won't Start
**Solution**:
1. Check Windows Defender/Antivirus (may need to allow the app)
2. Right-click → Run as Administrator
3. Check system requirements

## Tips & Best Practices

### Getting Better Results

1. **Be Specific with Topics**
   - ✗ Bad: "AI"
   - ✓ Good: "AI in Healthcare Diagnostics"

2. **Choose Appropriate Themes**
   - Academic topics → Basis or Academic themes
   - Business → Corporate Blue or Integral
   - Creative → Slice or Organic

3. **Export Planning**
   - Sharing with others → Export PDF
   - Older PowerPoint versions → Export PPT
   - Editing later → PPTX is best

### Resource Usage

- **Disk Space**: Each presentation ~20-50 MB
- **Generation Time**: 2-5 minutes depending on topic
- **Memory**: 500 MB - 1 GB during generation
