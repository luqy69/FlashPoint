### Window
- **850px width** (was 700px) - no more cutoff!

### New Step 2: Advanced Options
After entering your topic, you now choose:

#### 1. Slide Count
Select from dropdown: **10, 15, 20, 25, 30 slides**  
Default: 25

#### 2. Research Depth (3 Levels)
- **Level 1: Basic** - Simple, fun,  general audiences
- **Level 2: Professional** - Technical, data-driven, 2024-2026 studies
- **Level 3: Master Thesis** - Maximum 5000-word depth (DEFAULT)

### How It Works
Your selected research level determines which AI prompt template is used:
- `prompt_lvl_1` → Basic educational content
- `prompt_lvl_2` → Professional presentations  
- `prompt_lvl_3` → Expert-level depth
